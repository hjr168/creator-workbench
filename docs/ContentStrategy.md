# Content Strategy

## Content Positioning

The creator's content should come from a product manager's perspective.

Main directions:

- Product thinking
- AI tools
- Project management
- Personal growth
- Self-media experiments

## Platform Strategy

### WeChat Official Account

Best for:

- Long-form product thinking
- Structured methods
- Project reviews
- AI tool analysis

Suggested output:

- Article title
- Opening
- Core viewpoints
- Cases
- Closing
- Call to action

### Xiaohongshu

Best for:

- Strong titles
- Practical lists
- Personal experience
- Visual cards

Suggested output:

- High-click title
- Card outline
- Short paragraphs
- Save-worthy checklist

### Video Account

Best for:

- Short opinion videos
- Tool demonstrations
- Experience summaries

Suggested output:

- First 3-second hook
- Scene breakdown
- Voiceover script
- Closing call to action

### X

Best for:

- Sharp opinions
- Threaded observations
- English or bilingual experiments

Suggested output:

- Thread title
- 5 to 8 tweet points
- Closing prompt

## Topic Evaluation Questions

Before producing content, ask:

- Who is this topic for?
- What problem does it solve?
- What can the reader take away?
- Why is this worth writing now?
- Which platform is the best fit?
- Is this a one-off post or part of a series?
- What personal viewpoint makes it different?

## Angle Types

- Opinion
- Case study
- Tool review
- Methodology
- Project review
- Personal experiment
- Trend observation

## Title Strategies

- Pain-point title
- Contrast title
- Experience title
- List title
- Review title
- Question title
- Result-oriented title

## Outline Strategy

A useful long-form outline should include:

1. Opening hook
2. Context or problem
3. Core viewpoint
4. Supporting case or evidence
5. Practical method
6. Summary
7. Call to action

## Review Metrics

Record quantitative metrics:

- Reads
- Likes
- Favorites
- Comments
- Shares

Record qualitative review:

- Which topic direction performed well?
- Which title pattern worked?
- Which platform fit the topic best?
- What should be repeated?
- What should be avoided next time?

## Interaction Rule

The workbench should not skip directly from topic idea to full article generation.

The preferred flow is:

```text
idea -> topic -> angle -> title -> outline -> confirmed input -> tool generation
```
